\begin{exercise}
Let $P(t) = \sqrt{2} \left(\frac{4}{t^7} + 4t \right)^{8}$. Compute:

\[
\dd{t}P(t) = \answer{ 8\sqrt{2} \left(  \frac{4}{t^7} + 4t \right)^{7} \left(4-\frac{28}{t^8}\right)}
\]
\end{exercise}
