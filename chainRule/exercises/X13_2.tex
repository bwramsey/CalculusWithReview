	Let $y(u) = 4\sqrt{u^2-4u+1} - 6 \sqrt{u+1}$. 
	Compute:

	\[ y'(u) =\answer{ \left(2\right) \frac{2u-4}{\sqrt{u^2-4u+1}} - \frac{3}{\sqrt{u+1}} } \]
