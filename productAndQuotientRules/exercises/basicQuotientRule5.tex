\begin{exercise}
Let $R(u) = \frac{ 5 u^4+3 \sqrt[3]{u}}{-5 \sqrt[3]{u}-u^2}$. Compute:
\[
R'(u)
=\answer{ \frac{(-5 \sqrt[3]{u}-u^2)(20u^3+u^{-2/3})-(5 u^4+3 \sqrt[3]{u})(-\frac{5}{3}u^{-2/3}-2u)}{(-5 \sqrt[3]{u}-u^2)^2}   }
\]
\end{exercise}
