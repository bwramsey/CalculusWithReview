\begin{exercise}
Let $R(u) = \frac{ 5 u^4+3 \sqrt[3]{u}}{-5 \sqrt[3]{u}-u^2}$. Compute:
\[
R'(u)
=\answer{ ( (20u^3+u^(-2/3))(-5u^(1/3)-u^2)-(5u^4+3u^(1/3))(-(5/3)u^(-2/3)-2u))/(-5u^(1/3)-u^2)^2 }
\]
\end{exercise}
