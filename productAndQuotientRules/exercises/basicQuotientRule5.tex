\begin{exercise}

Let $R(u) = \frac{ 5 u^4+2 \sqrt{u}}{-4 \sqrt{u}-u^2}$. Compute:
\[
R'(u)
=\answer{ ( (20u^3+\frac{1}{\sqrt{u}})(-4\sqrt{u}-u^2)-(5 u^4+2 \sqrt{u})(-\frac{2}{\sqrt{u}}-2u))/(-4\sqrt{u}-u^2)^2 }
\]
